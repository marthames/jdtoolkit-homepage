Photography

As an aspiring visual journalist, some of my favorite scenes to capture include streetscapes and people authentically interacting in those scenes.

When creating imagery, I always aspire to:

  • go beyond the surface

  • consider how my camera and my view impact what I see

  • represent the actual circumstances I'm capturing

Here, for the purpose of this portion of the assignment, I fixed the lighting on the previously underexposed snowy street to bring out the reflective qualities of the snow and the streetlights.

As for the scene around the piano at Washington Square Park, I cropped the original image to really focus on the people interacting in the environment as opposed to incorporating the arch in the background.
