As we all know, the arguably most important event of this year will be the presidential election.

For this assignment, inspired by the idea to advertise an "event", I opted to call for the essential action of registering to vote. While not exactly how I wanted this to turn out, my intention was to incorporate visuals of former Vice President Joe Biden and President Trump, a font and coloring that stands out, and a handwritten quality that draws viewers in. Despite not being nearly as clean as I would've liked, it was a huge learning experience with Adobe Illustrator and graphic design.
